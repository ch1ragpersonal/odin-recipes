Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish consisting of layers of flat, wide pasta sheets alternated with various fillings, typically including rich meat sauces, cheeses, and sometimes vegetables. The most common filling is a ragù made from ground beef or pork, tomato sauce, and aromatic herbs like garlic, onions, and basil. Between these layers, creamy béchamel sauce or ricotta cheese is often added, providing a smooth, luscious texture. Mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses are typically sprinkled on top to create a golden, bubbly crust when baked. The dish is cooked in the oven until the flavours meld and the pasta softens, resulting in a hearty, comforting meal with a balance of savoury flavours and textures. Lasagna is a versatile dish, with variations including vegetarian and seafood versions, making it a popular favourite worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 500g Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • Minced Garlic
  • 2 cans crushed tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Lasagna Pasta sheets
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 180ºC
  2. Prep Meat Sauce
  3. Make Béchamel Sauce
  4. Cook Lasagna Sheets
  5. Assemble Lasagna
  6. Bake the Lasagna
  7. Rest and Serve
